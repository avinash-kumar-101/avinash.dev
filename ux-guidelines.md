# UX Guidelines

## Goal

Create a smooth, intuitive and professional experience.

---

## Navigation

- Navigation should be simple and predictable.
- Users should always know where they are.
- Keep important sections easy to access.

---

## Readability

- Maintain clear visual hierarchy.
- Use sufficient spacing.
- Avoid clutter.

---

## Interactions

- Buttons should clearly indicate their purpose.
- Hover effects should provide feedback.
- Keep interactions consistent.

---

## Scrolling Experience

- Sections should flow naturally.
- Avoid abrupt changes.
- Maintain generous whitespace.

---

## Accessibility

- Ensure sufficient contrast.
- Use meaningful labels.
- Keep text easy to read.

---

## Performance

- Avoid unnecessary animations.
- Optimize images.
- Keep loading experience smooth.

---

## Important Rules

- Simplicity over complexity.
- Consistency over creativity.
- Prioritize user experience above visual effects.
