# Portfolio Design System

## Role

Act as a senior UI/UX designer and frontend engineer.

## Goal

Maintain a premium and consistent design system throughout the entire portfolio website.

---

# Colors

- Use the same color palette across all sections.
- Do not introduce random colors.
- Maintain consistent primary, secondary, background, border, and text colors.
- Ensure sufficient contrast and readability.

---

# Typography

- Use the same font family throughout the website.
- Maintain a clear hierarchy.

## Heading Hierarchy

- H1
- H2
- H3

## Text Hierarchy

- Body Text
- Small Text

- Maintain consistent font weights and line heights.
- Avoid random font sizes.

---

# Spacing

- Maintain a consistent spacing system.
- Use uniform margins and paddings.
- Keep equal gaps between cards, sections, and components.

---

# Border Radius

Maintain consistent border radius values for:

- Buttons
- Cards
- Inputs
- Images

Avoid mixing different radius styles.

---

# Shadows

- Use soft and consistent shadows.
- Similar components should have similar shadow intensity.

---

# Buttons

## Primary Button

- Filled style.

## Secondary Button

- Outline or ghost style.

## Interactions

- Maintain consistent hover and transition effects.

---

# Cards

Project cards, skill cards, and experience cards should follow the same design language.

---

# Icons

- Use a single icon library.
- Maintain consistent icon sizes.

---

# Animations

- Use subtle animations.
- Keep duration and easing consistent.
- Avoid excessive effects.

---

# Responsive Design

- Maintain the same design language on desktop, tablet, and mobile.
- Avoid drastically changing layouts.

---

# Important Rules

- Treat the entire portfolio as one unified product.
- Prioritize consistency over creativity.
- Never introduce random styles.
